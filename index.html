About FoxArt

“We don’t make movies to make money, we make money to make more movies.” ― Walt Disney
Passion is what drives us all to dream more, do more and become more...

The idea of opening a high-end Media Studio in our town was in our minds right from the days we stepped in to the colorful world of media production. It took us a while to make our stand in the industry and sharpen our skills to put together a media studio that can deliver world class media works to our clients.

Today FoxArt is capable of delivering any level of media works right from weddings and events to commercial movies and music videos. Our team consists of exceptionally skilled professionals who've proven their expertise in the industry over the past few years. Our Services include:

  • Professional Media Editing
  • Event & Product Photography
  • Graphic designing
  • Cinematography
  • Training on various media courses

Uncompromised Technology. Cutting Edge Machines.

We at FoxArt believe technology plays a vital role in today's media production scenario. That's why we did the best investment we could in our machines. Our editing rigs consists of several Apple Mac Pro and Apple i-macs which are powered up for housing any latest graphics or VFX technology in the market. Our software expertise includes:

  • Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro, Adobe Aftereffects
  • Da Vinci Resolve with LUTs
  • Avid Media Composer, Canopus Edius, Sony Vegas Pro
  • Adobe Photoshop, Lightroom, Illustrator
  • RED Giant VFX Suite

We keep ourselves updated with the trend and the technology all the time. We promise you the best of the technology to be used to get your dream project a reality.
